# "Emily Overnell" — unverified

The name attached to the single-frontispiece 1912 Everett & Co. *Alice*. It comes **entirely
from a collector's reading of a signature** ("Overnell '12"); there is **no ODNB entry, no
library authority record, and no Lewis Carroll Society reference** to any artist of this name
[@wp_illustrators][@worthpoint_overnell]. A misread signature is plausible but unproven. Treat as
**Unknown / unverified** — not a confirmed pseudonym.

The "Gostling" lead is a confusion with **Mrs. J. C. Gorham** (*…Retold in Words of One
Syllable*, 1905), an unrelated text retelling [@wp_gorham].

**Confidence.** Low. This is a flagged, unresolved attribution.
