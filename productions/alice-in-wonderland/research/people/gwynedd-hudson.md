# Gwynedd M. Hudson, c.1882–1935

British illustrator; studied at Brighton School of Art and exhibited at the Royal Academy around
1912 [@askart_hudson][@wikidata_hudson]. She illustrated *Alice's Adventures in Wonderland* for
**Hodder & Stoughton** (1922, 12 colour plates), also issued as a Boots the Chemist edition
[@collectingalice_hudson]. Her biographical dates are genuinely uncertain; the best available
estimate is **c.1882–1935**. Note: a "1909–1935" rendering seen in some snippets refers to
