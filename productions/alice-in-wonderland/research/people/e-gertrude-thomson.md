# Emily Gertrude Thomson (E. Gertrude Thomson), 1850–1929

Born Glasgow; trained at Manchester School of Art [@wp_thomson]. Approached by Dodgson in 1878
after he admired her fairy drawings, she became a close friend until his death in 1898 and wrote
a short memoir of him. For the *Alice* line her documented contribution is the **front and back
covers of *The Nursery "Alice"* (1890)**, which she both designed and coloured — confirmed by the
V&A's title-page transcription ("The cover designed and coloured by E. Gertrude Thomson")
[@vam_nursery]. She also decorated Carroll's *Three Sunsets and Other Poems* (1898).

The frequently-copied claim (on her own Wikipedia page) that she **coloured the interior Tenniel
plates** of *The Nursery "Alice"* is **contradicted** by the V&A, the Morgan, and Cohen &
Wakeling; the interior colouring was Tenniel's, printed by Edmund Evans [@vam_nursery][@morgan_nursery][@wp_nursery].

**Confidence.** High on dates and on the corrected cover-only role.
