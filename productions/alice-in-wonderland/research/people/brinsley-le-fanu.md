# Brinsley Le Fanu, 1854/1855–1929

Irish illustrator (born Dublin), **son of the Gothic novelist Joseph Sheridan Le Fanu**, whose
books he often illustrated [@onlinebooks_lefanu][@just4fairytales_lefanu]. He illustrated the
**Esperanto** *Alice* — *La Aventuroj de Alicio en Mirlando*, British Esperanto Association,
1910. His **death year (1929) is firm**; the **birth year is unsettled** — library authority
files use 1854, artbiogs.co.uk lists 1855 [@onlinebooks_lefanu][@artbiogs_lefanu].

**Confidence.** Medium (birth-year split).
