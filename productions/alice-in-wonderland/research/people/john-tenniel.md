# Sir John Tenniel, 1820–1914

Principal political cartoonist of *Punch* for some fifty years; knighted 1893 (the first
illustrator to be so honoured) [@britannica_tenniel][@tate_tenniel]. He defined the canonical
*Alice* with **42 wood-engraved illustrations** for the 1865 first edition, cut by the **Dalziel
Brothers** [@aiw_tenniel]. His designs underlie the colourised *Nursery "Alice"* (1890) and the
Theaker re-colouring (1911), and remained the reference every later illustrator answered to. He
also illustrated *Through the Looking-Glass* (issued December 1871, title page dated 1872).

**Confidence.** High.
