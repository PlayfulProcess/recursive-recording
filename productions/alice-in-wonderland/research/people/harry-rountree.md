# Harry Rountree, 1878–1950

New-Zealand-born (Auckland) illustrator who moved to London in 1901 and worked in Britain
[@wp_rountree][@pook_rountree]. He illustrated *Alice* at least twice: a major colour-plate
edition for **Thomas Nelson** (1908) and the combined *Alice & Through the Looking-Glass* for
**Collins** (1928) [@wp_rountree][@collectingalice_rountree]. The "Nister / Children's Press"
attribution sometimes seen for the 1908 edition is incorrect.

**Confidence.** High on dates and on both editions.
