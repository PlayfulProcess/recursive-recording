# Bessie Pease Gutmann, 1876–1960

American illustrator (née Bessie Collins Pease), known for idealised images of children
[@wp_gutmann]. She illustrated *Alice's Adventures in Wonderland* for **Dodge Publishing Co.**,
New York, in **1907** (and *Through the Looking-Glass*, Dodge, 1909) [@nocloo_gutmann]. The
manifest's "1933" is a later **J. Coker & Co. (London)** reissue of her plates, not the original
date [@wp_gutmann].

**Confidence.** High.
