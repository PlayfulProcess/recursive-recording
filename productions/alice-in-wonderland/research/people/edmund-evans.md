# Edmund Evans, 1826–1905

The pre-eminent Victorian **colour wood-engraver and printer** (chromoxylography), famous for
the children's books of Walter Crane, Randolph Caldecott, and Kate Greenaway
[@wp_evans][@odnb_evans]. He **engraved and colour-printed *The Nursery "Alice"*** (1890),
working directly from Tenniel's colour proofs; he printed the first batch that Carroll rejected
as "too bright and gaudy" before the toned-down reprint [@morgan_nursery][@vam_nursery][@rareantique_nursery].

**Confidence.** High.
