# Harry George Theaker, 1873–1954

English illustrator, designer, painter, and art educator [@wp_theaker]. In **1911**, commissioned
by **Macmillan** and with **Tenniel's approval**, he hand-coloured **sixteen** of Tenniel's
*Wonderland* designs; his choice of a **blue dress** for Alice became the standard
[@panmac_theaker]. His work here is re-colouring of Tenniel's compositions, not a reillustration.

**Confidence.** High.
