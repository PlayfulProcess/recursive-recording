# W. H. Walker — identity disputed

Illustrator of the 1907 John Lane / Bodley Head *Alice* (8 colour plates + line drawings)
[@wp_illustrators][@marchpane_walker]. **No settled identity.** Catalogues variously attach the
edition to the British architect *William Henry Romaine-Walker* (1854–1940) or to the American
*Life*-magazine cartoonist *William Henry Walker* (1871–1938). A detailed investigation argues
the architect attribution is a conflation error and that the American cartoonist is most probable
on signature/monogram grounds — but it cannot be proven; other minor candidates exist
[@pdil_walker].

**Honest framing for the site:** "W. H. Walker; identity disputed, most likely the American
cartoonist William Henry Walker (1871–1938), but often conflated with architect W. H.
Romaine-Walker (1854–1940)." **Confidence: low.**
