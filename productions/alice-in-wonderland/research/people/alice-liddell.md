# Alice Liddell (later Hargreaves), 1852–1934

Middle daughter of **Henry George Liddell**, Dean of Christ Church; the child for whom Carroll
wrote the manuscript and to whom it was gifted [@wp_liddell][@encyclopedia_liddell]. She and her
sisters were frequent photographic subjects of Carroll's, and she repeatedly asked him to write
the story down [@umd_liddell]. She is the story's dedicatee and inspiration, though she
illustrated nothing herself.

**Confidence.** High.
