# Macmillan & Co.

Founded **1843** by brothers Daniel (1813–1857) and **Alexander Macmillan** (1818–1896)
[@britannica_macmillan][@panmac_175][@wp_amacmillan]. Carroll's publisher under a commission
model in which he funded and controlled production himself. Macmillan issued the canonical
Tenniel *Wonderland* (1865/66), *The Nursery "Alice"* (1890), and commissioned the Theaker
re-colouring of Tenniel's plates (1911).

**Confidence.** High.
