# Arthur Rackham, 1867–1939

Leading English illustrator of the golden age of book illustration. His 1907 *Alice* for
**William Heinemann** — **13 colour plates** plus line drawings — was the first major
post-copyright reillustration and the one most often judged an enduring rival to Tenniel
[@nocloo_rackham][@sancho_rackham]. Public-domain facsimile: Project Gutenberg #28885
[@gutenberg_28885].
