# Alice — illustrated by Harry Rountree (1928)

The New-Zealand-born, London-based illustrator **Harry Rountree** (1878–1950) illustrated *Alice*
more than once. The edition recorded here is the **1928** combined *Alice in Wonderland & Through
the Looking-Glass* from **Collins Clear-Type Press**, London/Glasgow [@wp_rountree][@collectingalice_rountree].

His **earlier and major** colour-plate *Alice* was published in **1908 by Thomas Nelson**,
Edinburgh — worth noting because it is frequently misattributed to "Nister / Children's Press"
[@wp_rountree][@pook_rountree]. A sometimes-cited "1916 collected edition" is **not corroborated**
in the sourced bibliography and should be treated as unverified.

**Confidence.** High on the 1928 Collins edition and on the existence of the 1908 Nelson edition;
the 1916 claim is unverified.
