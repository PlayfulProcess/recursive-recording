# Alice — illustrated by W. H. Walker (1907)

One of the first wave of new illustrated *Alice*s after the Tenniel-era copyright lapsed.
Published in **1907 by John Lane / The Bodley Head**, London, with **8 colour plates** plus
roughly 42 line illustrations [@wp_illustrators][@marchpane_walker].

The **identity of "W. H. Walker" is disputed.** Catalogues variously attach the edition to the
British architect *William Henry Romaine-Walker* (1854–1940) or to the American *Life*-magazine
cartoonist *William Henry Walker* (1871–1938); a detailed investigation argues the architect
attribution is a conflation error and that the American cartoonist is the most probable hand,
though not provable [@pdil_walker]. See `people/william-h-walker.md`.

**Confidence.** Edition (year/publisher) medium-high; illustrator identity low. Do **not**
publish firm lifespan dates for the artist.
