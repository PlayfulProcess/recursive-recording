# Alice — illustrated by Gwynedd M. Hudson (1922)

The British artist **Gwynedd M. Hudson** illustrated *Alice's Adventures in Wonderland* for
**Hodder & Stoughton**, London, in **1922**, with **12 colour plates**; it was also issued as a
**Boots the Chemist** (Nottingham) edition in collaboration with Hodder & Stoughton
[@askart_hudson][@collectingalice_hudson].

The artist's biographical dates are uncertain — best given as **c.1882–1935** — see
`people/gwynedd-hudson.md` [@wikidata_hudson][@askart_hudson].

**Confidence.** High on the edition (illustrator, year, publisher, plate count); low on the
artist's lifespan.
