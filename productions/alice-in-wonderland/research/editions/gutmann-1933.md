# Alice — illustrated by Bessie Pease Gutmann (1907; UK reissue 1933)

The American illustrator **Bessie Pease Gutmann** (1876–1960) illustrated *Alice's Adventures in
Wonderland* for **Dodge Publishing Co.**, New York, in **1907** (with a companion *Through the
Looking-Glass*, Dodge, 1909) [@wp_gutmann][@nocloo_gutmann].

The manifest's **"1933"** does not match the original: it corresponds to a later **J. Coker &
Co. Ltd. (London)** reissue of her illustrations (reprinted 1934), not the Dodge first edition
[@wp_gutmann]. The edition should be re-dated to **1907** (and the id `gutmann-1933` is
therefore misleading; consider `gutmann-1907`). If the repo specifically owns the 1933 Coker
plates, keep a 1933 note but record the original 1907 date.

**Confidence.** High on the illustrator, her dates, the 1907 Dodge first edition, and the 1933
Coker reissue.
