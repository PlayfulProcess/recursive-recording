# Alice — illustrated by Alice B. Woodward (1913)

**Alice Bolingbroke Woodward** (1862–1951) illustrated *Alice's Adventures in Wonderland* for
**G. Bell & Sons**, London, in Bell's **"Queen's Treasure Series,"** **1913**, with **16 colour
illustrations** [@wp_woodward][@bushey_woodward]. A handful of bookseller listings date it 1914,
likely a printing/release variation; 1913 is the standard date.

Note that the *Pinafore Picture Book* sometimes associated with her is a **different** Woodward
title, not this *Alice* [@wp_woodward].

**Confidence.** High on illustrator, dates, publisher, and series.
