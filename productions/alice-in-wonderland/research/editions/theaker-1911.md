# Alice — Tenniel re-coloured by Harry Theaker (1911)

In **1911**, **Macmillan** commissioned the artist-educator **Harry G. Theaker**, with
**Tenniel's approval**, to hand-colour **sixteen** of Tenniel's existing *Wonderland*
compositions [@wp_theaker][@panmac_theaker]. These are not new drawings but new colour applied
to the canonical Tenniel designs. Theaker's choice of a **blue dress** for Alice became the
iconic standard, and his coloured Tenniel plates appeared in Macmillan editions through roughly
1911–1930 [@panmac_theaker].

**Confidence.** High on the colourist, the 1911 date, the publisher (Macmillan), and the fact
that this is re-colouring of Tenniel rather than a reillustration.
