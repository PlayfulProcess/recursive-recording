# Alice — "Emily Overnell" (1912)

A minor 1912 edition published by **Everett and Company** (Everett's Library, 42 Essex Street,
London W.C.). Its illustration is limited to a **single frontispiece** incorporating the title
page; the only trace of the artist's name is a **signature reading "Overnell '12"**
[@wp_illustrators][@worthpoint_overnell].

**"Emily Overnell" is an unverified identity.** No reputable biographical source — no ODNB, no
library authority record, no Lewis Carroll Society entry — documents an artist by this name. The
name derives entirely from a collector's reading of the frontispiece signature; a misread
signature is plausible but unproven. It should be treated as **Unknown / unverified**, not as a
confirmed pseudonym [@wp_illustrators].

A common lead — "Mrs Henry / J. C. Gostling" — is a **confusion** with Mrs. J. C. *Gorham*,
author of *Alice's Adventures in Wonderland Retold in Words of One Syllable* (1905, A. L. Burt,
New York; illustrated by J. Watson Davis). That is a separate text-retelling lineage with **no
connection** to the Overnell edition [@wp_gorham].

**Confidence.** Low. Publication facts (Everett & Co., 1912, single frontispiece) are citable;
the person's identity is genuinely unknown.
