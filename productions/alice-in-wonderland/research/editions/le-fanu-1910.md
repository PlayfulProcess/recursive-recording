# Alice — illustrated by Brinsley Le Fanu (1910)

Brinsley Le Fanu illustrated the **Esperanto** translation of *Alice*, *La Aventuroj de Alicio
en Mirlando* (translated by E. L. Kearney), published in London by the **British Esperanto
Association** in **1910** [@wp_illustrators]. This is a notable, easily-missed point: it is the
Esperanto edition, not an English-language *Alice*.

The illustrator was **George Brinsley Sheridan Le Fanu**, son of the Gothic novelist Joseph
Sheridan Le Fanu (see `people/brinsley-le-fanu.md`) [@onlinebooks_lefanu][@just4fairytales_lefanu].

**Confidence.** Medium. Year and publisher are attested; the artist's birth year (1854 vs 1855)
is unsettled across authorities.
